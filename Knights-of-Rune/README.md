Description: 
Knights of Rune is a small MMO where players take on the role of a dragon exploring two biomes, a forest and a desert, separated by a central river and bridge. Players can gather resources by chopping trees and mining ores, and battle three types of skeleton enemies. All assets and scripts were created in-house using the Unity engine. 

Engine used: Unity 2022.3.25f1
